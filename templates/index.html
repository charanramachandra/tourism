About Us

some Words About Us

Welcome to our J&I Travels, your ultimate guide to exploring the best of Japan in one day. We specialize in curated itineraries for unforgettable day trips to Hiroshima, Kyoto, Mt. Fuji, Nara, Osaka, and Tokyo. Whether you're a first-time visitor or a seasoned traveler, let us help you discover Japan's rich history, culture, and natural beauty through our expertly crafted experiences.

Services

Adventure Offer

Hiroshima:

Visit Hiroshima Peace Memorial Park, Shukkeien Garden, Hiroshima Castle, and Orizuru Tower. Enjoy lunch at Okonomimura and explore Miyajima Island's Itsukushima Shrine. Savor local cuisine for dinner.

Kyoto:

Explore Kinkaku-ji (Golden Pavilion), Fushimi Inari Shrine, and Arashiyama Bamboo Grove. Enjoy lunch at Nishiki Market and experience Gion's traditional atmosphere in the evening.

Mt. Fuji:

Hike or take a scenic drive to Mt. Fuji's 5th Station, visit Chureito Pagoda, explore Lake Kawaguchi, and relax in a local onsen.

Nara:

Visit Todai-ji Temple, feed deer in Nara Park, explore Kasuga Taisha Shrine, and stroll through the historic Naramachi district.

Osaka:

Explore Osaka Castle, stroll through Dotonbori for street food, visit Shitennoji Temple, and experience the views from Umeda Sky Building.

Tokyo:

Visit Senso-ji Temple in Asakusa, explore Shibuya Crossing, experience the Meiji Shrine in Harajuku, enjoy Tokyo Tower's views, and savor sushi at Tsukiji Outer Market.

Your Special One-Day Tour

Hiroshima: Explore Peace Memorial Park, a poignant reminder of history. Visit Shukkeien Garden for tranquility amidst urban hustle.

Kyoto: Admire Kinkaku-ji (Golden Pavilion) and the spiritual Fushimi Inari Shrine. Stroll through Arashiyama's Bamboo Grove for serene beauty.

Mt. Fuji: Experience breathtaking views from Mt. Fuji's 5th Station. Capture iconic photos at Chureito Pagoda with Mt. Fuji in the backdrop.

Nara: Encounter Todai-ji Temple's Great Buddha and friendly deer in Nara Park. Wander through historic Naramachi district for cultural immersion.

Osaka: Discover Osaka Castle's history and vibrant Dotonbori for street food delights. Visit Shitennoji Temple for spiritual tranquility.

Tokyo: Explore Senso-ji Temple in Asakusa and bustling Shibuya Crossing. Experience the tranquility of Meiji Shrine in Harajuku and iconic Tokyo Tower views.
